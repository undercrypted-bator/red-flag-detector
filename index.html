What Are Relationship Red Flags?

Red flags are behaviors that suggest unhealthy dynamics such as manipulation, disrespect, emotional neglect, control, dishonesty, or even abuse. Identifying these signs early can help protect your emotional well-being and support healthier relationship decisions.

Not every concerning behavior means a relationship is doomed. Some issues arise from stress, miscommunication, or personal growth challenges. That is why this tool evaluates behaviors across multiple psychological categories before offering a classification.

How This Tool Works

The Relationship Red Flag Detector evaluates behaviors across multiple dimensions, including emotional maturity, consistency, trust, respect, accountability, and trajectory of change. Some behaviors are automatically classified as serious red flags, particularly those involving physical harm or severe emotional abuse.

Other behaviors may fall into a yellow category if they represent warning signs that require communication and boundary-setting rather than immediate termination of the relationship.

If no concerning patterns are detected, the tool will indicate a green flag, suggesting that the described behavior aligns with healthy relationship principles.
