How This Tool Works

The Relationship Red Flag Detector helps users reflect on common behaviors in romantic relationships. By typing one specific action or pattern, the tool evaluates it based on general relationship psychology principles.

What Are Red Flags?

Red flags are behaviors that indicate unhealthy dynamics such as lack of communication, emotional neglect, manipulation, or disrespect. Identifying red flags early can help individuals make better decisions and maintain healthier boundaries.
